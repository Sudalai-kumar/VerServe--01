Brand Identity: VeriServe

## Brand Name
VeriServe Always written in CamelCase (Capital V and S). Never written as "veriserve" or "Veri-Serve."

### Acceptable Usage
VeriServe

VERISERVE (for headers only)

### Unacceptable Usage
VeryServe (misspelling)

Veriserve (lowercase 's')

V-Serve (abbreviation)

## Visual Identity
### Color Palette
Primary (Trust Green): #00A86B — Represents growth and safety.

Secondary (Action Blue): #1E90FF — Used for "Volunteer Now" buttons.

Warning (Scam Red): #FF4C4C — Used for flagged/unverified content.

Background (Clean Slate): #F8F9FA — To keep the focus on the data.

### Tone of Voice
Empathetic: We care about the community.

Authoritative: We are the experts on what is a "safe" request.

Clear: No technical jargon; make it easy for a student or a retiree to understand.
